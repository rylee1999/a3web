Investors

Scrooge McDuck

Scrooge McDuck is a Scottish billionaire known for his unmatched wealth and frugal demeanor. Starting from humble beginnings in Dismal Downs, Scotland, Scrooge built his fortune through successful ventures in mining and treasure hunting, earning him the title of the "World's Richest Duck." Despite his gruff exterior, Scrooge's adventures, alongside his nephews and loyal companions, showcase a complex character with a soft spot for family and a zest for life's thrilling pursuits.

Eugene Harold Krabs

Mr. Eugene Krabs, the penny-pinching crustacean from Bikini Bottom, is the shrewd and money-loving owner of the Krusty Krab. Mr. Krabs is characterized by his insatiable desire for profits, a love for counting money, and his famous catchphrase, "Arr, me hearties!" Despite his obsession with wealth, Mr. Krabs occasionally reveals a softer side, particularly when it comes to his daughter, Pearl, showcasing a more nuanced and complex character beneath his money-focused exterior.

Arlene Dickinson

Arlene Dickinson is a prominent Canadian businesswoman, entrepreneur, and television personality, best known for her role on the hit TV show "Dragons' Den." Born on October 8, 1956, in South Africa, she moved to Canada and co-founded Venture Communications, a highly successful marketing communications agency. With her sharp business acumen, Dickinson has become a respected figure in the Canadian business landscape and a source of inspiration for aspiring entrepreneurs.

Alex Miller

Alex Miller is the esteemed CEO of Esri Canada, a leading geospatial technology company. With a rich background in geography and a passion for leveraging spatial intelligence, Miller has been instrumental in guiding Esri Canada to the forefront of the geospatial industry. Under his leadership, the company has consistently pioneered innovative solutions that harness the power of location-based data for diverse sectors, solidifying Miller's reputation as a visionary leader in the field.
